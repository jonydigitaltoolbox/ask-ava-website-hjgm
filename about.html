Our Story

Growing up, I struggled with self-confidence in my studies. I found that personalized support made all the difference in my academic journey. Now, I aim to empower high school students in Montreal to overcome their challenges and build confidence through tailored tutoring.

At Ask Ava, we are dedicated to fostering an environment where high school students can thrive academically and personally. Our mission is to build confidence and skills that last a lifetime, equipping students for success in their studies and beyond.
